Mapo Tofu

Description

Ma po tofu (麻婆豆腐) is a classic tofu and ground meat dish fry from the Sichuan province. Ground Sichuan peppercorns and doubanjiang are the two key ingredients to an authentic and delicious Mapo Tofu.

Ingredients

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablesppon ginger, minced
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 8-10 dried Chinese chili peppers
  • 2 tablespoons doubanjiang
  • 8 ounces ground pork
  • 1 pound silken tofu
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch

Steps

  1. Toast the chilies for 45s in skillet with 2 tablespoons of oil. Add the sichuan peppercorns for 15s. Remove from heat and set aside.
  2. Add the remaining oil and add the ginger. After 45s, add in the garlic and the whites of the green onions. After 30s, add in the ground pork and fry until cooked through.
  3. Add the doubanjiang and cook for 45s, then add in the chicken broth. Once the chicken broth is simmering, add the tofu, along with the chilies and peppercorns from step 1.
  4. Make a cornstarch slurry with the cornstarch and 1-2 tablespoons of cold water. Add the slurry in three stages, waiting for the water to boil before adding more. Let everything simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Right before removing from heat, add the sesame oil. Then plate the Mapo Tofu with the scallion greens on top.
